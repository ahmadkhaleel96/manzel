Goals, Vision, Mission

Our Vision

Manzel aims to bridge universities and market jobs by transforming theoretical knowledge into a poignant interactive easy-to-learn experiment to build a solid foundation that helps students and engineers to gain more experiences before engaging with a real job in less time. Manzel has three main goals: 1- Interactive and enjoyable content. 2- Easy to learn. 3- Accurate educational knowledge.

What is Manzel?

About Us

Manzel is a Virtual Educational Platform that helps students and engineers gain more experience and skills required for market jobs using artificial intelligence and advanced visualization technologies to make teaching more provocative and accurate.
